What happens during training?
  1. Extracts 7 features from equipment maintenance history
  2. Splits data: 80% training, 20% testing
  3. Trains 3 models: Gradient Boosting, XGBoost, Random Forest
  4. Evaluates on TEST set (never seen during training)
  5. Performs 5-fold cross-validation
  6. Selects best performing model
  7. Saves model for predictions
