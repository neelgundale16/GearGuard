# GearGuard - Product Roadmap

## Product Vision
Enterprise-grade SaaS platform for predictive maintenance management

## Target Market
- Manufacturing plants
- Facility management companies
- Industrial equipment operators

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
1. **User Engagement:**
   - Daily Active Users (DAU)
   - Monthly Active Users (MAU)
   - Session duration

2. **Business Metrics:**
   - Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
   - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
   - Lifetime Value (LTV)
   - Churn Rate

3. **Product Metrics:**
   - Equipment tracked
   - Maintenance requests processed
   - ML prediction accuracy
   - Cost savings for customers

| ML Predictions | 1000 | 3 | 80% | 5 | 480 |
| Mobile App | 800 | 2 | 70% | 8 | 140 |

## Go-to-Market Strategy

1. **Phase 1:** Free tier for SMBs (0-10 equipment)
2. **Phase 2:** Paid tiers for mid-market
3. **Phase 3:** Enterprise custom solutions

## Pricing Strategy

- **Free:** Up to 10 equipment
- **Starter ($49/mo):** Up to 50 equipment
- **Professional ($199/mo):** Up to 200 equipment
- **Enterprise (Custom):** Unlimited + white-label
